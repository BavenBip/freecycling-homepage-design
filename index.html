Free to Give

Have items but you don’t want to throw away? We’ll do it for free!

Join our network of over 2000 local communities across the globe, uniting eco-minded individuals to embrace the spirit of sustainability to preserve valuable resources.

About Freecycling!


Freecycling! is a nonprofit promoting sustainability, born when individuals saw usable items being discarded and connected with nearby nonprofits to find them new homes.

Our Mission


Our mission is to cultivate global sharing to minimize waste and conserve resources. Unite individuals worldwide for environmental responsibility through generosity and collaboration.

How does it work?


When you register on Freecycling! at the top right, you can join local groups or create nearby friend circles. Share items you want to give away or receive, coordinate pick-up with members, rescuing items from landfills, giving them a second chance.
